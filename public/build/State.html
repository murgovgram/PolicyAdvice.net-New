What Are State Minimum Requirements?

Texas minimum auto insurance requires you to buy a mandatory insurance amount of $30,000/$60,000/$25,000. This means that if you get into an accident, you can avail $30,000 coverage for bodily injury for one person, $60,000 coverage for more than one person, and $25,0000 for property damage for each accident.

If you are a resident of Texas, your insurance policy must include two forms of liability coverage – property damage and bodily injury liability.

If you are found responsible for an accident, the property damage coverage will safeguard your assets. This can prove to be particularly helpful if you cause damage to any other vehicle or property.

Moreover, if you get into an accident, the bodily injury liability covers expenses of bodily harm that may have been inflicted on others.
